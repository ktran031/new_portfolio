About Me

Hello!

My name is Khoi Tran and I’m a web designer/developer that is currently looking for new and challenging projects.
I began studying Web Design at an SEO company, landing my first job as a WordPress Web Designer.
I recently graduated from the Front-End Web Development program at General Assembly.
I graduated from the University of California, Riverside with a B.A. in Sociology, and found my passion for web design/development
reignited after helping several colleagues with their own up and coming websites.
When I’m not hard at work developing new projects, you can find me at the gym trying to maintain an active
and healthy lifestyle where I can challenge myself physically and mentally!
